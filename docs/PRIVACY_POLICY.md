# Privacy Policy

Catholic Fasting is a local-first devotional app.

## Data stored on device

The app stores the following on your device:

- profile and fasting-rule settings
- observance completion history
- Friday notes
- intermittent fasting sessions and active timer state
- reminder preferences
- premium planning and reflection entries

## Sharing and backups

- This Android release does not include backup, export, import, or household-share flows.
- Android system backup is disabled for the app.
- The app does not automatically upload your observance data to a remote server.

## Tracking and ads

- The app does not include third-party ad SDKs.
- The app does not use ad-tracking identifiers.

## Billing

- Google Play Billing is used for subscriptions and optional support purchases.
- Purchase state is handled through Google Play for the signed-in Play account.
